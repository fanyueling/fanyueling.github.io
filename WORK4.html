Smart trash bag 智能垃圾袋

Basic Info
Tool：Xmind / Illustrator / PowerPoint
Team member:4
Target group:Children and youth aged 3-18
Type:Service process system combining online and offline
Service Process:
Each garbage bag has a QR code. You can upload and record the data to the campus management system by tearing a garbage bag. Then the device displays the number of remaining garbage bags. When the balance is insufficient, it will remind you to add it in time. In this way, the garbage generation of a group can be recorded, and users can be more intuitively understood. In addition, there is a device for scanning the QR code in the trash can, which can also play a role of recording feedback to detect the actual use of the trash bag and upload the data to the supervision system. Through the feedback on the use of the garbage bag, you can see which part of the month has a lot of consumption, so you can adjust your behavior accordingly. Through monthly assessment, from the use of the garbage bag of the device and the garbage feedback of the trash can, a value assessment is made for the corresponding user group, and a corresponding reward and punishment mechanism is made.
