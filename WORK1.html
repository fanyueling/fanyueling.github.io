The Robot 机器人

Basic Info
Tool：processsing
Team member:1
Interaction description:
Robot A controls the robot B by typing on the keyboard. The antennas of B receive radio waves while flickering to generate rolling signal waves. The abdomen generates random color patches with a trigonometric function as the trajectory. Move the mouse to B ’s head, and the B robot will “tremble”; move the mouse to B ’s abdomen, and the abdomen screen will change to “I LOVE PROCESSING”.
